Come as a guest. Leave feeling like family.

Genesis Gardens and Campsite is a place built from the heart — where lush gardens, warm hospitality, and a table always set for you come together, just minutes from Kampala.

Book a visit See what we offer

Vanessa Ngango opened her home to the world because she believed everyone deserves a place that feels like somewhere they belong. What started as a passion for welcoming people became Genesis — a sanctuary of gardens, good food, and genuine care, just off Kampala Road in Mukono-Seeta.

However you want to spend your time here, we'll make it feel like home.

Stay with us

Whether you're pitching a tent under the stars or settling in for a few nights, you'll sleep somewhere that feels cared for — because it is.

Celebrate here

Weddings, parties, graduations, corporate gatherings. Our gardens become yours. We handle the details with the same love we'd give our own.

Come for the day

You don't need to stay overnight to feel the difference. Come for a picnic, a family afternoon, or a quiet moment away from the city. You're always welcome.

Eat well

From full buffets and sit-down meals to open-fire BBQ evenings — food at Genesis is cooked with care and served with warmth. That's just how we do things.
